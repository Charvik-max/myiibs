Admission Process

  1. Fill out the online application form on the IIBS website.
  2. Submit required documents and entrance exam scores.
  3. Shortlisted candidates will be called for Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI).
  4. Final selection based on academic record, entrance score, GD & PI performance.
